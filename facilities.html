Academic

Computer Lab
The School has four well equipped air conditioned labs with around 200 computers. The labs are also equipped with smart boards and projectors to make the learning experience better.
Library
The school has three high-tech computeriezed resource centers coupled with knowledge hub complete with an extensive collection of National and International books, CD's, Journals, and Videos. The school takes great pride in maintaining a book colloection over 35000 books.
Science Lab
Hi-tech computerized Physics, Chemistry, Biology Labs with combined class rooms for working out projects in a throughly scientific environment.
Language Lab
An essential tool for making english an individual part of students life and providing a global exposure, the lab is fully euipped with recorders and phones for self directed learning.

Sports

Outdoor Games
The school has two football grounds, three cricket grounds, five basketball courts, four long tenis courts, two volleyball and handball grounds. The school team wins various tournaments around the year. Teams represent school in lot of prestigious state and national tournaments.
Indoor Activities
The school has lot of indoor activities such as swimming, brilliards, shooting, badminton, skating, chess, judo, karate, gym, and squash. Students participate is all the state and national level tournaments in the country.

Co-Curricular

The school has 3 state of art sound proof dance rooms, with regular trainers of bharat natyam, odissi, and kathak. The school believes in provoding rich cultural values to all its student.

The school students have been making their mark on the national quiz platform all thanks to the quiz club. The quiz club helps prepare out students for various different types of quizes and also erriches their aptitude.
